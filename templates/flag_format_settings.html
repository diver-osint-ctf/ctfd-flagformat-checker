Regular Expression Examples

Common Patterns
  • flag\{.*\} - Matches: flag{anything}
  • CTF\{[a-zA-Z0-9_]+\} - Matches: CTF{alphanumeric_underscore}
  • [A-Z]{3}\{[a-f0-9]{32}\} - Matches: ABC{32-char-hex}
  • flag\{[0-9a-f]{8}-[0-9a-f]{4}-[0-9a-f]{4}-[0-9a-f]{4}-[0-9a-f]{12}\} - UUID format
Important Notes
  • Use \{ and \} to match literal braces
  • Pattern must match the entire flag (not just a part)
  • Case-sensitive matching is used
  • Invalid regex patterns will prevent saving
  • Empty pattern disables format validation
